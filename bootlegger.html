Bootlegger
Den in the heart of the City
☆☆☆☆☆

A sultry prohibition inspired den in the heart of the City, The Bootlegger is a drinking establishment primed for London's retro-lovers.
Speakeasy themed, and harking back to the underground glamour of the 1920s, The Bootlegger puts two fingers up the the prohibition in a bid to bring London's fun-time folk a unique dose of vintage inspired boozing, with live music, American eats and vintage adornments aplenty.
From lavish cocktails, to live jazz bands that make it all too easy to while the night away,
The Bootlegger is a premium haunt, and just the place to drop a few clams
